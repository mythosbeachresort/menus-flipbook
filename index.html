How to use (GitHub Pages safe)

  1. Upload your PDFs to menus/ (e.g., menus/dinner.pdf), then open:
    index.html?pdf=menus/dinner.pdf
  2. Or export images named 1.jpg, 2.jpg, … into menus/dinner/, then open:
    index.html?base=menus/dinner/&ext=jpg

Important: Use relative paths like menus/dinner.pdf (no leading slash).
